What's this project about?

Key Motivations

I am a daily driver myself driving the roads of San Francisco, so needless to say, I have experienced a lot on the road. I've seen everything from texting and driving to hit and runs. Through this project, I want to both inform and remind drivers that there is risk when driving, and that this risk can easily be mitigated by paying some more attention to the road.


The Data

I got the data from a government database. The data is actively updated and maintained by the city of Chicago. It includes all reported crashes starting from 2015-2017 to today. I want to mention once again that countless crashes go unreported, so this data is a small sample of a very large problem in urban society. It is currently May 2024, so for my research and analysis, I only looked at the data from 2018 to 2023.

The data is publicly accessible and can be found here.


Project Objectives

  • Bring attention to the quantity of accidents that occur
  • Understand how visibility affects the risk of being involved in an accident
  • Show main things to look out for to avoid accidents
  • Highlight the damage and danger of being in an accident

Design and Methodology

This site is a template from HTML5UP. Specifically the Massively template. I replaced the image containers with svg containers, and used d3js to create vizualizations of the data.
